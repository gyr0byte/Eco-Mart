From a Small Startup to 100 Million
Trees

The 10-Tree Promise Tentree was founded in 2012 by two Canadian friends and established a radical formula of ecommerce: Tentree plants ten trees with every product being purchased. What began as a modest hoodie company has expanded to become a worldwide restoration movement.

Green Friday vs. Black Friday Although other stores are promoting over-consumption, Tentree launched a tradition known as Green Friday. They do not simply pursue profits, but instead they take advantage of the peak shopping days to finance immense reforestation, at one point planting 1 million trees on one weekend.

E-Commerce Transparency They apply technology in closing the gap between impact and shopping. Each EcoMart-type customer receives:
A Trackable Code: Track your specific GPS location on trees in Madagascar or Nepal.
A Impact Wallet: See your own forest grow.

By providing a credit option through their Reshop program, Tentree will close the Loop by keeping clothing out of landfills and on bodies, resulting in a circular economy.

The Result Tentree has planted more than 105 million trees, a fact that demonstrates that ecommerce can be the major driver of planet recovery. At EcoMart we turn to them as evidence that each purchase can be a restoration tool.
