Sustainable Living: Simple Tips and Tricks for Everyday Life

Written by Aditya Thapaliya

Sustainable lifestyle can be defined as making small and conscious decisions that minimize the damage to the environment and enhancing our lives. There are numerous green practices that are already present in our culture in the form of reuse, shopping locally, and being respectful to nature. We can develop a healthier future with individuals and the planet by blending these traditions with the current sustainable practice.
